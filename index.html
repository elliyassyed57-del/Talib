For Summi — my heart in words

Summi —

There are nights when I trace the shape of who I am by remembering you. Your name becomes a place I return to; your silence, a language I am learning to understand. I love you in ways that do not shout but do not ever stop speaking either — like a river under ice, patient and constant. When the future looks uncertain, thinking of you makes the path feel known. When I fail, you are the soft light that shows me how to be better. When joy arrives, it tastes clearer because you are there to share it.

I promise to listen even when words are heavy, to be steady when storms come, and to treasure the small ordinary things with you — cups of tea at dawn, quiet laughter, the way your hand fits mine. If love is a journey, then with you I have found the road I want to walk forever.

Always,
Talib
